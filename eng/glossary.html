Glossary

Science Fiction:
Art genre describing a futuristic world with newly developed technologies.
Cyberpunk:
A movement and sub-genre of science fiction, born in the 1970s, depicting a world city, technological dependence, transhumanism, a dark future often with political overtones.
French Touch:
Movement and sub-genre of house music, born in France in the 90s, it is a music with multiple influences (funk, disco...)
La Treille:
Neighbourhood of about 820 inhabitants, located in Marseille, France.
Banksy:
British artist born in 1974, graffiti artist known for his urban stencils. We know his real identity.
Lorenzo Quinn:
Italian artist born in 1966, painter and sculptor known for his ecological commitment.
Web:
World Wide Web, allows access to different sites and content on the Internet using a browser (Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Saphari...)
AI (Artificial Intelligence):
A set of programmed machines whose purpose is to imitate human intelligence and behaviour.
Deep Fake:
AI-based technique of digitally replacing one face with another.
Dystopian Future:
A fictional story about a society in the future that has gone wrong: the misuse of new technologies or the establishment of a totalitarian power are two themes very present in this kind of story.
